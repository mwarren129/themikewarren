Public Speaking

35 Years of Dealmaking. Delivered From the Stage.

Mike Warren has spoken to audiences across the country on business acquisition, creative financing, and building wealth through ownership. His talks aren't theory. They're built on 5,000+ real transactions, three private equity firms, and the hard lessons most coaches never learned because they never did the deals.

"I don't teach from slides. I teach from deals I've closed, mistakes I've made, and businesses I still own today."

What Mike Speaks On

Keynote

Business Acquisition & Entrepreneurship

How everyday professionals can escape the corporate grind by buying existing, profitable businesses instead of starting from scratch. The acquisition path most people never consider.

Workshop

Creative Financing & SBA Strategies

The 80/10/10 deal structure, SBA lending mechanics, seller financing negotiations, and the financing strategies that separate successful buyers from everyone else.

Keynote

The Bulletproof Methodology

Mike's proprietary system for evaluating deals with criteria far above bank minimums. DCR, DSCR, cash flow floors, working capital requirements, and the scoring framework that protects buyers from bad deals.

Keynote / Panel

Building Wealth Through Business Ownership

Why the wealthiest people in America own businesses, not stocks. How to build generational wealth through acquisition, not entrepreneurship from zero.

Specialty

Profiting From Debts on Property

Mike's RPLP program and the overlooked world of real property lien investing. How debts attached to property create investment opportunities most people never see.

Specialty

Mechanics' Liens & Tax Liens

The technical side of lien investing. How mechanics' liens and tax liens work, how they get resolved, and where the profit opportunities live in the process.
