The Bulletproof Methodology

Most people who try to buy a business fail before they even get to the closing table. They overpay, they skip due diligence, or they trust projections instead of verified cash flow. Mike spent three decades watching deals collapse for the same preventable reasons, so he built a system to eliminate them.

The Bulletproof Methodology isn't theory. It's a set of non-negotiable financial criteria refined across thousands of real transactions. Every deal must pass a stress test before a dollar changes hands. Debt coverage, working capital, earnings multiples, and cash flow floors, all verified, all documented, all designed to protect the buyer from day one.
